Performance Marketing

Google & Meta Campaign Framework

Structuring precise geographical and search setups with advanced GA4 analytics tags.

The Challenge

Local campaigns often leak spend by targeting overly broad regions and failing to isolate high-intent keywords. Without exact event tracking, measuring concrete cost-per-acquisition (CPA) is impossible.

The Solution

We designed a geographic search network template with granular targeting filters. The setup is configured with native GA4 events mapping lead completions, allowing campaigns to isolate and scale only profitable demographics.

Project Highlights

~25%

Projected savings in wasted ad spend

  • Ad Networks: Google & Meta
  • Tracking: GA4 Leads & Events
  • CRM Link: HubSpot Auto-Feed
